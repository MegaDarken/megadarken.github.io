I have found that looking for usable resources online has seemed more obtuse as time has gone on. Many old sites would just be [shareware dinosaur icons](https://web.archive.org/web/20021118151407/http://www.geocities.com:80/Heartland/6459/dinoicons.html), or [collections of MIDIs](https://web.archive.org/web/20091022185353/http://geocities.com/spazz501.geo/page4/horrormidis.html). You could philosophize about why you see this less, the larger platforms are less welcoming of downloading things. Regardless of the cause; I want to see more useful resources of all kinds, so I thought that at the very least I should compile and present what I currently have for others in a similar position to me.
